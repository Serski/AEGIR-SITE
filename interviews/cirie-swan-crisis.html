Cirie-Swan Crisis: Pregnant heiress feared trapped in Nova space

By Lysette Marr, CTTS Senior Gossip Correspondent

Darling readers, hold onto your hoverchairs: the glittering bliss of “The Empire Couple” has been eclipsed by a cosmic catastrophe. Signals now suggest Aurelia Cirie-Swan is pinned inside or near Nova Confederation territory—and she is pregnant with twins.

Initial reports claiming Aurelia had cleared Nova space were exposed as a massive signal confusion with an Orion cargo convoy. The cold, hard truth remains: the third-highest Orion executive is stranded within a zone that could ignite at any moment.

Alarm bells and emergency fleets

The moment the gravity of the situation hit OISF High Command Hall, alarm bells rattled every spire on Ida. CEO Alpheus Orion Cirie and CSO Darius Cirie-Swan reportedly led the charge for an immediate, high-priority extraction. One Orion board member confessed, “The fate of the future of Orion is literally hanging in the balance.”

A secret revealed: the heir crisis

In the white-hot pressure cooker of the crisis, a carefully guarded secret burst into the public sphere: Aurelia is expecting twin boys. The rescue shifted from high-stakes extraction to galactic imperative overnight. Two elite wings mobilized instantly: the 2nd Security Echelon Star Group and Darius’ personal 88th Security Echelon, code-named Inquisitor.

Where are the fleets?

As of this feed, the locations of the dispatched echelons are shrouded in the deepest black of space. Orion has imposed a code-zero blackout, refusing to confirm movements or Aurelia’s status. Are they already breaching Nova pickets? Has Darius plunged into the danger zone to retrieve his pregnant wife?

The sector holds its breath. All of us at Closer-To-The-Stars are praying for the safe return of our luminous heiress. May the Inquisitor bring her and her unborn children home before the war drums begin.
